FAQ

Questions Answered

The quiz uses a trained Random Forest classifier built with scikit-learn. Your answers are vectorised and fed into a model trained on 2,400+ anonymised student profiles. The backend is a Flask API — the ML model runs server-side and returns a probability distribution across career clusters.
The database is updated every Sunday via a scraper that pulls from LinkedIn, Internshala, and local Jaipur tech company job boards. You'll always see listings posted within the last 7 days.
Absolutely. You can retake it as many times as you like. Many users take it twice — once at the start of a semester and once at the end — to see how their profile evolves as they gain skills.
The current model achieves 94% accuracy on our validation set. We measure this by following up with users 6 months after their quiz — 94% report feeling well-matched to their recommended path.
The career quiz and basic roadmap are completely free. Premium features (1-on-1 mentorship, weekly internship alerts, resume review) are available at ₹299/month — less than a Jaipur biryani dinner.
